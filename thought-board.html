Thought board instructions

*ALL THOUGHTS WILL BE PUBLIC ONCE PLACED*
Type your thought in the text box and click add. Drag your thought to your desired location on the thought board and click submit. This will publicly publish your thought in that position. The board is self moderating: if you think your thought is better than a thought currently on the board, place your thought on top of it. Theoretically, the thought board will one day display only the best thoughts. Each thought cannot be more than 50 characters. Each user is currently limited to a maximum of 3 thoughts within a 24 hour period.
