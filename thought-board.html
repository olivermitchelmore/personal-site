Thought board instructions

*ALL THOUGHTS WILL BE PUBLIC ONCE PLACED*
Type your thought in the text box and click add. Move your thought to your desired location on the thought board and click place. This will publically publish your thought in that position. If you think your thought is better than a thought currently on the board, place your thought on top of it. Theoretically, the thought board will one day display only the best thoughts. Each thought must be below 100 characters. Each user is currently limited to a maximum of 3 thoughts in a 24 hour period.
